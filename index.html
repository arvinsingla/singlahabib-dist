Things to do while you're in Toronto

  • #1 Shopping on Queen Street West

    Men and women clothing stores are hard to resist on this trip! The best shopping is between Bathurst and Bellwoods.

  • #2 Ripleys Aquarium of Canada

    Recently opened, this aquarium is tons of fun - especially for families. You can even pet a stingray! What!

  • #3 TIFF Bell Lightbox

    The mecca of the Toronto Film Festival! If you have a few hours to spare in a dark room, this place hosts the best of art house cinema. Also, they are hosting a Stanley Kubrick exhibition!

  • #4 AGO (Art Gallery of Ontario)

    One of Toronto’s most prestigious art galleries, hosting a number a fascinating exhibits. Always a great visit.
